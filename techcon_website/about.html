History

Since its founding in 2010, TechCon has grown from a grassroots meetup into one of the region's most influential technology conferences. Each year we've brought together engineers, founders, researchers, and students to share breakthroughs and build community.

Over the years we've expanded our program to include workshops, hands-on labs, and an innovation fair that highlights startups and open-source projects.

Mission

TechCon exists to accelerate knowledge-sharing and to create opportunities for collaboration across disciplines. We aim to make advanced technology accessible, encourage ethical practices, and support diverse voices in tech.

Our core principles:

  • Share practical, production-ready knowledge.
  • Promote inclusivity and diverse perspectives.
  • Foster mentorship and community-driven learning.

Join us in building a future where technology serves everyone.
